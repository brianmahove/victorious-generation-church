About Our Pastor

Prophet Kudakwashe Jose

Zimbabwean born, Prophet Kudakwashe Jose was called into ministry at the tender age of 17. That journey began with a call to seek the Lord alone on the mountains of Zimbiru, Zimbabwe, where he ended up living for three years.

The Lord supernaturally provided for his daily needs and trained him until he was ordained into ministry from 2017. He is used mightily by God in powerful miracles, signs and wonders — the blind see, the lame walk and the dead are raised.
